SwapMyShow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

SwapMyShow lets people list event tickets they can no longer use and chat with other users to swap them. This page explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Account details: your name, email address, and phone number, collected when you sign up (or via Google sign-in on the web). Your password is stored only as a salted hash — never in plain text.
  • Content you create: ticket listings (event, venue, city, date, seats, prices, an optional ticket screenshot you upload) and the chat messages you exchange with other users about a swap.
  • Activity: swaps you take part in and the ratings you give and receive, which power the trust badges shown in the app.

How it is used

  • To operate the service: show your listings to other users, deliver your chat messages, and display trust signals (verified badges, ratings, completed-swap counts).
  • To secure your account: one-time codes are emailed (or texted) to verify your address or phone. Codes are stored only as hashes and expire quickly.
  • We do not sell your data, show third-party ads, or process payments — any payment for a swap is settled directly between the two users, outside the app.

What other users can see

Your display name, trust badges, rating, and completed-swap count appear with your listings and chats. Your email, phone number, and password are never shown to other users by the app.

Storage & third parties

Data is stored on Cloudflare's platform (Workers, D1 database, and R2 for uploaded images). Verification emails are delivered via Brevo. Google sign-in, when used, is provided by Google Identity Services.

Your choices

You can edit or remove your listings at any time from the app. To request deletion of your account and its data, contact us at the address below.
